ErgoDox

This is a keyboard that I had been planning on building ever since I saw that you could on ergodox.io. It'll tell you exactly what you need, links to get the PCB, list of components required and an assembly guide. I had a lot of fun building this, especially being able to smell the solder fumes again.

It features a split, ortholinear layout to help out with your ergonomics whilst using the keyboard.

Features

  • Micro-USB Pro Micro Controller
  • Ortholinear Layout
  • 76 - 80 Key Layout
  • MX Switch Compatible
  • Flex material for your friends

About

For my specific build I had chosen to get this PCB off Amazon US, although it doens't seem to be in stock as of April 2020. I had also chosen to use Gateron Yellow switches for this build. I wanted something that had a higher actuation force and linear. They won't make your fingers ripped like the Cherry Black switches, or even Gateron Browns, but I swear the Yellows are heavier...
