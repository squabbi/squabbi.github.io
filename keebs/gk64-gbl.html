GK-64

My first 'custom' mechanical keyboard that I bought from Chinese eCommerce giant, TaoBao. I've had my share of gaming mechanical keyboards, but these images on the internet had caught my eye and I was hungry for a smaller mechanical keyboard, perhaps this 60%, as even the 10-keyless layouts were not something I wanted to use. I needed something slim, sleek and compact. Although it might look the part, it did not weigh anything like that.

Features

  • USB Type-C Connector
  • 60% Staggered Layout with nice arrow key placement
  • Word's most annoying right Shift size
  • MX Switch Compatible
  • RGB Lighting

About

Again, my first custom mechanical keyboard here. I liked that it still had the arrow keys as well as a mostly standard sized layout, say execpt for the left Shift key. The aluminium base is super heavy, and that helps with dampening the sound that it makes when you type. Not the quietest, but make things click very sounding very precise. It's perhaps the most sturdy feeling keyboard that I've owned. Very pleased with it, although I may prefer a few more keys now.
